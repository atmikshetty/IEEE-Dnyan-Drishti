About

We the esteemed IEEE SFIT Student Branch from St. Francis Institute Of Technology, Mumbai, proudly present our inaugural national-level hackathon. Since it is 25th college anniversary and the college has received NAAC AAA+ accreditation, we are conducting a Hardware and Software Hackathon . A total of 110 teams will compete for a cash prize of Rs. 3,00,000/- .
